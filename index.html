Who We Are




Welcome to SAASTEPS!


Our company is structured to empower businesses by connecting them with the right partners. With every guided step you decide to take with SAASTEPS, we will help fill in any process gaps, implement desired software solutions, or aid in training your team.


The Founder and CEO has been in the SAAS arena for 14+ years. From this, Tim has learned many valuable nuances within the space, turning them into ways to promote efficiencies. He founded this company with a focus on supporting vendors that are 100% native to the Salesforce platform. SAASTEPS thrives to be a Thought Leader in the industry with strong partnerships, and efficient cloud-based solutions.

Our Approach




We know how obstacles, challenges, hurdles, or whatever you choose to call them, can be somewhat of a hinderance. From time to time, an outside perspective helps relieve this. So, this is where we come in!


Our approach is to connect you with some of our most powerful partners, and to help you step up to your ultimate cloud platform. With our expertise, we will analyze, design, and configure the process path best suited for your needs. Whether it is filling in those overlooked process gaps, generating more leads, increasing your ROI, or creating an entire end-to-end 100% native platform, we will guide you and your team to the last implementation.
